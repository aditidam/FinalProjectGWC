Our Mission

To increase awareness about environmentally harmful products and companies.

Most people have been taught that individuals are at fault for all the environmental pollution. If you take shorter showers, people say, you can make the world a better place. While small actions positively do affect the world, corporations can quickly negate these actions by manufacturing products that are detrimental to our planet's ecosystem. Hopefully, Corporate Crusher will enlighten consumers about the effects their purchases have on the evironment.
