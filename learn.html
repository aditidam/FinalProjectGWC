Unfriendly Products:

Dawn Plus Oxi Dishwashing Soap

Dawn Plus Oxi Dishwashing Soap contains methylisothiazolinone (MIT). It is a chemical that is toxic and can end up in aquatic environments. In addition, it has virulent effects if it contacts the skin or eyes of animals.

Neutrogena Foaming Face Scrub

Neutrogena foaming soap contains microbeads as an exfoliant, however these microbeads are known as polyethylene. They transport phenanthrene which is a by product of a fossil fuel, and a toxic ocean pollutant. These microbeads cannot be broken down. Even more concerning, it ends up in the sewage system and remain there which causes it to end up in bodies of waters and can be eaten by marine animals. This product deeply affects human and the food chain.

Windex

Windex contains detrimental chemicals such as Ammonium Hydroxide. This creates acute aquatic toxicity. Ammonium Hydroxide is known as a respiratory irritant that has the ability to damage eyesight as well as skin in humans and other animals.

Eco-Friendly Substitutes:

Dr.Bronner’s Castile Soap

Dr.Bronner’s Castile Soap is known to be an eco-friendly product, made from olive oil, coconut oil, and other vegetable oils.This product receives its name since it is from the Castile region of Spain. This product comes with multiple uses such as a body wash, facial cleanser, dishwashing soap, household cleaner, etc.Since this soap is organic and biodegradable it can be used outdoors without putting freshwater biomes in risk.

Lush Body & Lip Scrubs

Lush has a reputation of being an eco-friendly company. Unlike many other scrubs in the market, Lush scrubs have natural exfoliants, not microbeads. They use sugar or salt as exfoliants. Their products are biodegradable, organic, and handmade. Even their packaging is eco-friendly. Their bottles are BPA- free and made from 100% post-consumer recycled (PCR) plastic. Even their gifts are wrapped in 100% recycled paper and are packaged with biodegradable filler..

Aspen Window Cleaning

Aspen Window cleaning also known as a green cleaner is a known eco-friendly product. Not only is this brand known for window cleaning but it is also used for gutter cleaning.
