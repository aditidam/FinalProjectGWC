Unfriendly Products:

Dawn Plus Oxi Dishwashing Soap

Dawn Plus Oxi Dishwashing Soap contains methylisothiazolinone, a toxic chemical that can end up in aquatic environments. In addition, it has virulent effects on skin and eyes.

Neutrogena Foaming Face Scrub

Neutrogena Foaming Face Scrub contains microbeads as an exfoliant. These toxic polyethylene microbeads transport phenanthrene (a byproduct of a fossil fuel) and end up being eaten by aquatic animals.

Windex

Windex contains many toxic chemicals such as Ammonium Hydroxide. Ammonium Hydroxide is a respiratory irritant that has the ability to damage eyesight and skin in animals.

Eco-Friendly Substitutes:

Dr. Bronner's Castile Soap

Dr. Bronner’s Castile Soap is made from olive oil, coconut oil, and other vegetable oils. Since this soap is organic and biodegradable, it can be used without endangering aquatic animals.

Lush Body & Lip Scrub

Unlike some other scrubs on the market, Lush scrubs use natural exfoliants, not microbeads. The packaging is BPA-free and made from 100% post-consumer recycled plastic.

AspenClean Window Cleaner

AspenClean Window Cleaner is a known eco-friendly product. Not only is this brand known for window cleaning but it is also used for gutter cleaning.
