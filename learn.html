Learn More About These Products

Dawn Plus Oxi Dishwashing Soap

Dawn Plus Oxi Dishwashing soap contains methylisothiazolinone (MIT). It is a chemical that is toxic and can end up in aquatic environments. In addition, it has virulent effects if it contacts the skin or eyes of animals.

Neutrogena Foaming Face Scrub

Neutrogena foaming soap contains microbeads as an exfoliant, however these microbeads are known as polyethylene. They transport phenanthrene which is a by product of a fossil fuel, and a toxic ocean pollutant. These microbeads cannot be broken down. Even more concerning, it ends up in the sewage system and remain there which causes it to end up in bodies of waters and can be eaten by marine animals. This product deeply affects humans and the food chain.
